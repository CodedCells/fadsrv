Cookie Help

Why use cookies?

Cookies are required to perform some automated tasks involving your account on Fur Affinity.

grab_post

Fur Affinity prevents any Mature or Adult rated posts from being displayed, unless you are logged in and have them enabled under your account settings.

If you have your favourites private, the program can only access them with your session cookies.

user_stats

As above, information based on last post date is affected if posts are hidden

Warning

Do not share your cookies, as they can be used to access your account.

If you suspect someone else may have your cookies, IMMEDIATELY:

  1. Go to Fur Affinity's Active Sessions Page
  2. Hit all the log out buttons

You should have to log back in, and you'll need to get your new cookies.

Setting up secret.json

If there isn't a file at data/secret.json, create an empty text file with that name.

Example structure, where the values would be replaced:

{
	"a": "your_a_cookie",
	"b": "your_b_cookie"
}

Get your cookies

Chrome

  1. Open Developer tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Application tab
  3. Sidebar: under Storage > Cookies > https://www.furaffinity.net
  4. Copy and Paste the A and B cookies into secret.json
  5. Save the file

Firefox

  1. Open Web Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Storage tab
  3. Sidebar: under Cookies > https://www.furaffinity.net
  4. Copy and Paste the A and B cookies into secret.json
  5. Save the file
